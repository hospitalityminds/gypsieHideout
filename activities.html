Activities

We want you to have your best moments at absolute peace. Gypsie's Hideout provides you with the best opportunities to make your dream holiday come true. Book with us to have a memorable vacation of your lifetime.
Our main attractions are:


  • 3 Bedrooms

  • 4 Bathrooms

  • Swimming Pool

  • Nature Side

  • Bird Watching

  • Beach Access

  • Indoor Games

Bedrooms

Gypsie Hideout has 3 spacious bedrooms to accomodate all of our guests comfortably and in luxury.

Fully Furnished
Moroccan Decor

Bathrooms

All of our bedrooms have attached ensuite bathrooms so that each guest has their own privacy. There is also a separate bathroom on the ground floor.

Pool

Guests can chill and cool down in the beautiful private pool or read a book or have a drink by the poolside. The leisure is endless.

Nature Tour

Be blanketed in Alibaug's splendid natural surroundings of bird songs and diverse trees. Or soak in the sweet smell of the ocean.

Bird Watching

Whip out your binoculars and spend an afternoon away roaming in the surrounding trees, following chirps of birds.

Beaches

Visit Alibaug's famous beaches for an amazing evening. The beach is at a walking distance from the villa and makes for a perfect evening swim.

Indoor Games

When you are tired of swimming and bird watching, head back inside the villa and spend some quality time with your freinds & family members playing carrom or snooker.

Gypsie's Hideout

This bohemian style, spacious villa is beautifully accented with Moroccan decor which will please you every moment. Don't be surprised if you hear the waves or catch a whiff of sand during your stay at Gypsies' Hideout - you're just about a stone's throw away from the beach.
