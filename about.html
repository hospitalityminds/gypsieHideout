THE STAY

The villa has 3 bedrooms of which 2 are on the ground floor and one is on the first floor. All the bedrooms have ACs and attached ensuite separate bathrooms standard.

The bedroom on the first floor is the master bedroom with an ensuite television. The place also has an open terrace where guests can sit and enjoy the surrounding nature and birds in peace. Surrounded by various trees and the breath of fresh air by the sea, your stay will be a very calming experience for sure!
